Some Words About Me

Graduated in 2022 from the Centria University of Applied Sciences of Kokkola town, where completed the degree program in Bachelor of Engineering, Information Technology. At the moment, I position myself as a Front-end Developer.

After graduation, my choice was made in favor of Web Development, where I completely plunged headlong. I started with the basic things - HTML and CSS. I really liked making the layout of the pages - worked with mockups in Figma. Later, I mastered the skills of working with the Bootstrap framework for fast and adaptive layout. I am also improving my skills with JavaScript as well as libraries such as React and Jquery, and other stuff. In addition, I have work experience with small libraries - List.js and SweetAlert2.

I have a great desire to become a member of the IT family, where I will be useful and will be able to make a significant contribution to the common cause, where I will constantly develop myself and my skills and be an integral part of one whole mechanism.
